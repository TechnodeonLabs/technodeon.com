Account Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Account

At Technodeon, we respect your data privacy rights. If you wish to delete your Class Tracker Jr account and all associated personal data, please follow the instructions below:

Account Deletion via Email

To request the deletion of your account:

  1. Send an email to support@technodeon.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request - Class Tracker Jr"
  3. Include your account email address in the email body
  4. We will process your request within 5 business days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted
Email Support

Important Information

Please note the following regarding account deletion:

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers
  • Any saved class information, schedules, or notes will be lost
  • Account deletion requests are typically processed within 24-48 hours

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues deleting your account or have questions about our data practices, please contact our support team:

  • Email: support@technodeon.com
  • Response Time: Within 2 business days
