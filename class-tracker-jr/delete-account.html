How to Delete Your Account

At Technodeon, we respect your data privacy rights and make account deletion simple and straightforward. If you wish to delete your Class Tracker Jr account and associated data, follow the instructions below.

1 Send Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account, send an email with the following details:

  • To: support@technodeon.com
  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - Class Tracker Jr
  • Body: Please include the email address associated with your account
Send Deletion Request

2 Verification Process

Our team will verify your account ownership and begin the deletion process. You may receive a verification email to confirm your identity before proceeding.

3 Confirmation

Once your account has been successfully deleted, you will receive a confirmation email. This typically happens within 48 hours of your request.

! Important Information

Please be aware of the following regarding account deletion:

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All your personal data will be completely removed from our systems
  • Your class information, schedules, and notes will no longer be accessible
  • Your user profile and preferences will be permanently deleted
